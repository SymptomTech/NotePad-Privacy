Privacy Policy

NotePad by SymptomTech

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

SymptomTech ("we," "us," or "our") operates the NotePad mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using NotePad, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Privacy-First Approach: NotePad is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All your notes, folders, and content are stored locally on your device. All AI features (speech-to-text and photo-to-text) process data on-device only. We do not have access to your personal data or notes.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Notes and Content: All text content you create, including titles, body text, and checklist items
  • Folders and Categories: Your organizational structure, folder names, and color preferences
  • App Settings: Theme preferences (light/dark/system), font selections, sort order, and feature toggles
  • Backup Files: When you create backups, they are stored on your device or your chosen storage location (Downloads folder, external storage, etc.)
  • Speech Recognition Models: Downloaded language models for speech-to-text (stored in app's private directory)
  • Pin Status: Which notes you have pinned
  • Archive Status: Which notes and folders are archived

Important: We do not have access to any of this data. It exists only on your device.

1.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be crystal clear about what we do not collect:

  • Your notes and content: Never accessed, transmitted, or stored on external servers
  • Voice recordings: Audio captured during speech-to-text is processed locally and immediately discarded after transcription
  • Photos: Images captured or selected for photo-to-text are processed locally and immediately discarded after text extraction
  • Transcriptions: All transcribed text from voice or images is stored only in your local notes
  • Personal information: No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
  • Account data: The App does not require registration or account creation
  • Location data: We do not access or collect your location
  • Usage analytics: We do not track how you use the App or collect usage statistics
  • Crash reports: No automatic crash reporting or analytics are collected

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Google AdMob (Free Version Only)

The free version of the App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain information to deliver personalized advertisements. This information may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage information (when ads are viewed)
  • General location information (derived from IP address)

For more information about how Google AdMob collects and processes data:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AdMob Partner Policies: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903

Note: Premium users who purchase ad-free experience will not be subject to AdMob data collection.

2.2 Google Play Billing

When you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed through Google Play's billing system. Google Play collects and processes payment information according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment details.

Available In-App Purchases:

  • Premium Ad-Free ($3.99 CAD): Removes ads, unlocks unlimited folders, full color palette (24 colors), archive access, and backup/restore features
  • Speech-to-Text ($2.99 CAD): Unlock offline voice typing with support for 11 languages
  • Photo-to-Text ($2.99 CAD): Unlock OCR functionality to extract text from photos

2.3 Google ML Kit (Photo-to-Text Feature)

If you purchase and use the Photo-to-Text feature:

  • Text recognition is performed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit
  • The ML Kit model (~10MB) is automatically downloaded to your device on first use
  • Photos are processed locally and are never uploaded to Google servers or our servers
  • No images are stored by the app or transmitted anywhere
  • The feature works offline after the initial model download

Learn more about ML Kit: https://developers.google.com/ml-kit

2.4 Vosk Speech Recognition (Speech-to-Text Feature)

If you purchase and use the Speech-to-Text feature:

  • Speech recognition is performed entirely offline on your device
  • Language models are hosted on our GitHub repository: github.com/SymptomTech
  • You download only the language models you choose (each model is 30-50MB)
  • Models are stored in the app's private directory on your device
  • Audio is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after transcription
  • No audio recordings are saved, transmitted, or uploaded anywhere
  • The feature works completely offline

Supported Languages: English (US), Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Italian, Russian, Japanese, Chinese, Hindi, and Indian English

4. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect or have access to your notes, content, or personal information, we cannot use it. The only data processing that occurs is:

4.1 On-Device Processing Only

  • Note Storage: Your notes are stored in the app's local database (Room/SQLite) on your device
  • Speech-to-Text: Audio is processed by Vosk models running entirely on your device
  • Photo-to-Text: Images are processed by ML Kit models running entirely on your device
  • Backup/Restore: Data is exported/imported directly to/from your device's storage as ZIP files

4.2 What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not analyze your notes or content
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except AdMob for free users, as disclosed)
  • We do not use your data for marketing or advertising (beyond AdMob's own operations)
  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not create user profiles

5. Data Security

5.1 On-Device Security

  • All app data is protected by Android's built-in security features
  • Data is stored in the app's private directory, inaccessible to other apps
  • No user authentication required (no passwords to compromise)
  • Backup files are standard ZIP files that you control

5.2 Network Security

  • No note content is ever transmitted over the network
  • The only network connections are:
    • AdMob ad delivery (free version only)
    • Google Play Billing for purchases
    • ML Kit model download (one-time, ~10MB, Photo-to-Text feature)
    • Vosk model downloads from GitHub (optional, for languages you choose)
  • All network connections use secure HTTPS protocols

5.3 Backup Security

  • Backup files are created locally and stored where you choose
  • We do not have access to your backup files
  • Backup security depends on where you choose to store them
  • We recommend storing backups in secure locations (password-protected cloud storage, encrypted drives, etc.)

6. Children's Privacy

NotePad does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed to work entirely offline with local storage, making it safe for all ages. Parents and guardians can confidently allow children to use the app as no personal data is collected or transmitted.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. However, given our privacy-first design, it is highly unlikely any data has been collected.
