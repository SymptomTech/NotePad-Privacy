Privacy Policy

NotePad by SymptomTech

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

SymptomTech ("we," "us," or "our") operates the NotePad mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using NotePad, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Privacy-First Approach: NotePad is designed with your privacy in mind. All your notes, checklists, and voice recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Notes and Checklists: All text content you create, including titles, body text, and checklist items
  • Folders and Categories: Your organizational structure, folder names, and color preferences
  • App Settings: Theme preferences, font selections, sort order, and other customization options
  • Backup Files: When you create backups, they are stored on your device or your chosen cloud service (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)

1.2 Speech-to-Text Feature

Privacy Guarantee: When you use the speech-to-text feature:

  • 100% Offline Processing: All voice recognition is performed locally on your device using downloaded language models
  • No Audio Recording Stored: Voice input is converted to text in real-time and the audio is immediately discarded
  • No Cloud Processing: Your voice never leaves your device or gets uploaded to any server
  • No Third-Party Access: No external services have access to your voice data
  • Language Models: Downloaded language packs are stored locally on your device

1.3 Technical Information

We may collect minimal technical information to improve the App:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version (for compatibility)
  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics about app crashes and performance issues (anonymous, no personal data)
  • Google Play Services: Information required for in-app purchases and ad display (handled by Google)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide core features like note-taking, organization, and speech-to-text
  • App Improvement: To identify and fix bugs, improve performance, and enhance user experience
  • In-App Purchases: To process your purchases of premium features (Remove Ads, Speech-to-Text)
  • Advertising: To display ads (only if you haven't purchased the Remove Ads feature)

Important: We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Permissions We Request

NotePad requests the following permissions:

3.1 Microphone Permission (RECORD_AUDIO)

  • Purpose: Required for the speech-to-text feature
  • When Requested: Only when you tap the microphone button after purchasing the speech-to-text feature
  • Optional: You can use the app without granting this permission (speech-to-text will not be available)
  • Privacy: Audio is processed locally in real-time and never stored or transmitted

3.2 Internet Permission

  • Purpose: Required for:
    • Displaying advertisements (in free version)
    • Processing in-app purchases (Google Play Billing)
    • Downloading speech-to-text language packs (one-time per language)
  • Not Used For: Uploading your notes, voice data, or personal information

3.3 Storage Permission

  • Purpose: To create and restore backup files
  • User Control: You choose when to create backups and where to save them

4. Third-Party Services

NotePad integrates with the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Play Services

  • Purpose: In-app purchase processing
  • Information Shared: Purchase transaction data (handled by Google)
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google AdMob

  • Purpose: Displaying advertisements (only in free version)
  • Information Shared: Anonymous advertising identifiers for ad targeting
  • Opt-Out: Purchase the "Remove Ads" feature to stop all advertising
  • Privacy Policy: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

4.3 Vosk Speech Recognition

  • Purpose: Offline speech-to-text processing
  • Information Shared: None - Vosk runs entirely on your device
  • Privacy: No cloud processing, no data transmission
  • Open Source: Vosk Project

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

  • All your notes are stored in a local database on your device
  • Data is stored in the app's private directory (inaccessible to other apps)
  • If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device

5.2 Backups

  • Backups are created only when you explicitly choose to create them
  • You control where backups are saved (local storage, Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)
  • We do not have access to your backup files
  • Backup security depends on where you choose to store them

5.3 Security Measures

  • App data is protected by Android's built-in security features
  • In-app purchases are secured by Google Play's encryption
  • No user authentication required (no passwords stored)

6. Children's Privacy

NotePad does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is designed to work entirely offline with local storage, making it safe for all ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Control

  • Access: You can access all your data within the app at any time
  • Export: Create backups to export your data
  • Delete: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data

7.2 Permissions

  • You can revoke microphone permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → NotePad → Permissions
  • The app will continue to function without microphone access (speech-to-text unavailable)

7.3 Advertising

  • Purchase "Remove Ads" to permanently stop all advertisements
  • Adjust ad personalization in your Google account settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Notifying you via the app (for significant changes)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.

9. International Users

NotePad is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, data transfer laws do not apply. Your data never leaves your device except when:

  • You explicitly create a backup and save it to a cloud service of your choice
  • Anonymous analytics are sent to Google (for crash reports and performance monitoring)
  • In-app purchase transactions are processed by Google Play

10. Data Retention

Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app

Purchase Records: Maintained by Google Play in accordance with their policies

Analytics Data: Anonymous technical data may be retained by Google for up to 26 months

By using NotePad, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
